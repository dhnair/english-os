Objective: A complete diagnostic of your English OS so far. This exam tests Data Types (Phase 2), Verb States (Phase 3), Logic Gates (Phase 4), and the Master Architecture (Phase 5).

Instructions: Fix the "Null Pointer" and "Data Type" exceptions. Write the clean code below.

3. (Corporate): "Can you send the updated financial datas to she?"

4. (Logistics): "There are too many traffics on the supply route today."

5. (Hospitality): "The hotel staff is preparing the room for they."
